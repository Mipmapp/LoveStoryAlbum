# Design Guidelines: Romantic Digital Scrapbook Website

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based:** Handmade scrapbook aesthetic brought to digital life - nostalgic, personal, and heartfelt.

## Visual Theme & Aesthetic

**Color Palette:**
- Soft beige, blush pink, cream, brown, and pastel tones
- Warm, romantic, nostalgic color scheme

**Textures & Materials:**
- Paper textures throughout
- Torn edges on photos/sections
- Tape strips (washi tape aesthetic)
- Polaroid frames with shadows
- Notebook line backgrounds
- Hand-drawn doodles (hearts, arrows, stars)

**Typography:**
- Handwritten fonts: "Dancing Script", "Indie Flower" for personal touches, love notes, captions
- Clean sans-serif: "Poppins" for body text and readability
- Mix handwritten and clean fonts to create scrapbook authenticity

## Layout System

**Spacing:** Organic, slightly irregular spacing to mimic handmade scrapbook
- Photos should be slightly tilted (2-8 degrees rotation)
- Asymmetric placement with soft shadows
- Generous padding to let elements breathe

**Component Positioning:**
- Polaroids: Overlapping, taped appearance with drop shadows
- Sticky notes: Floating with soft shadows, slight rotations
- Elements should feel "pasted" onto pages, not perfectly aligned

## Animations & Interactions

**Page Transitions:**
- Paper flip animation between sections
- Smooth, gentle transitions (800-1200ms duration)

**Element Animations:**
- Fade-in photos as if being "pasted" onto page
- Handwriting animation for love notes (typewriter effect)
- Floating doodles with subtle movement
- Sticky notes pop-in with slight bounce

**Interactive Elements:**
- Floating heart cursor trail
- Polaroid shuffle animation on page load
- Flip page arrows at bottom corners for navigation
- Hover effects: gentle scale, rotation, or glow
- Click sounds: page flip, soft clicks

**Parallax Effects:**
- Doodles move slightly when scrolling
- Subtle depth with background elements

## Images

**Required Images:**
- Cover page: Vintage/aged paper background texture
- Polaroid photos: 8-10 placeholder frames for couple photos (slightly aged filter)
- Paper textures: Multiple variations for different pages
- Envelope: Folded paper graphic for letter section
- Doodles: Hand-drawn hearts, stars, arrows (SVG or PNG with transparency)
- Washi tape: Decorative tape strips in pastel colors

**Image Treatment:**
- Polaroids: White borders, slight yellowing/vintage filter
- Soft vignettes on backgrounds
- Subtle paper grain overlays
- Warm color temperature throughout

## Special Features

- Background music player with soft romantic ambiance
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
- Sound effects (page turn, envelope open, note stick)
- Hidden clickable elements for surprises
- Smooth scrolling or page-by-page navigation

## Emotional Design Goal
Every element should feel handcrafted with love - imperfect, personal, and deeply heartfelt. The site should evoke smiles, nostalgia, and emotional connection through its warm, intimate aesthetic.
